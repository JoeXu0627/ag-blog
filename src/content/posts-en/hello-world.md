This is the first post on this blog.

I've worked at iFLYTEK Research, Dedao, and ByteDance — on model training, big data engineering, and B2B consulting. I've since left to build my own startup, making AI products for the global market.

This blog will document what I learn and think about across three areas:

- **LLMs**: models, agents, and the engineering work of shipping them
- **SEO**: hands-on lessons in content growth and search traffic
- **Audio/video technology**: the pitfalls and solutions I accumulate while building my product

Writing serves two purposes for me: turning scattered practice into reusable knowledge, and nudging myself to keep showing up and engaging with the world.
