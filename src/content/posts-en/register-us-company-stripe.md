## Why You Need a US Company

Before taking an AI product global, you'll need to sort out a few things:

1. **Collecting payments**: To charge users worldwide, the go-to payment processor is Stripe — and it requires a company entity in the US (or another supported region) plus a local bank account.
2. **Getting a credit card**: Run all necessary company expenses through a credit card to keep tax filing simple. With Mercury, you can also issue sub-cards to your employees — quick and convenient.
3. **Fundraising**: If the business takes off and you want to raise from USD funds, a US entity is a must.

Below is a fairly standard path: you can start collecting payments within a week, and get your tax ID (allowing withdrawals to your bank card) in 2-3 weeks.

## Incorporating the Company

Tax rates vary by state, and some states — like Delaware — are tax-free, which is why companies are typically incorporated there. The easiest route is Stripe Atlas, which handles the whole process online from anywhere.

### Choosing a Company Type

If you're starting lean, a single-member LLC is the way to go — annual tax filing is much simpler. The C-Corp option is mainly for setting up a Cayman structure.
